TONY JIANG - Executive Director

Tony Jiang, executive director of Link People Capital, was a member of the board of directors of Centaline Real Estate（中原地产）and was the founder, first employee, and GM of 6,000 employee Baoyuan Real Estate, a Centaline sister company. While the GM at Baoyuan, Baoyuan earned 60 billion in sales on 1000+ developer projects between 2007 to 2016. After leaving Baoyuan, he invested in 18 sales representative companies throughout China.

ISAAC ROSENTHAL - Operating Director

Isaac Rosenthal, operating director of Link People Capital is responsible for investor relations, corporate strategy, internal SOPs and IT. Isaac is a serial entrepreneur who started a real estate leasing and management company Link People at the age of 23. Link People had 300 share flat apartments with 1500 tenants under management rented to young expat professionals in downtown Shanghai. In 2016, Link People was sold to Dothink Group, a top 50, Hong Kong listed Chinese real estate developer. Since 2017, Isaac, focused on capital fundraising and real estate acquisition projects for Dothink before joining Link People Capital.

CHARLES FANG - Acquisition Director

Charles Fang acquisition director at Link People Capital is responsible for project underwriting, audit and acquisition. A graduate of Singapore Management University, Charles has passed CFA level 2 exam. Charles was the head of investment and M&A of a Shanghai Stock Exchange publicly listed company. While at HNA he led 10 billion RMB of mergers and acquisitions of residential, commercial and entertainment real estate. Additionally, he internally restructured 5 billion RMB of related group assets and spearheaded the establishment of many new subsidiary companies at HNA. He has over 10 years of real estate M and A experience and 5 years of experience in corporate governance, corporate structuring and financial management.

CHEN CHENG - Investment Manager

Chen Cheng, investment manager at Link People Capital, is responsible for investor and government relations. He was the former vice president of Minsheng Bank – Jingan sub branch. He has participated in the acquisition of 3 billion of real estate assets, 800 million of real estate NPLs, and fundraising of 1 billion. He has 5 years of experience in asset management, acquisition of non-performing assets and investment fundraising.
